This if the JustAFK player options file. It stores settings for each player with custom settings. 

There are 4 pieces of information about each player: 
- afkexempt: whether the player is exempt from being automatically set AFK or not 
- seehidden: whether the player cans see hidden AFK players or not 
- kickexempt: whether the player is exempt from being kicked or not 
- lightningexempt: whether the player is exempt from being struck by lightning when going AFK or not
